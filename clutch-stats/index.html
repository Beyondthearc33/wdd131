Player Bio:

Russell Westbrook is one of the most electrifying point guards in NBA history. Known for his explosive athleticism, relentless energy, and unmatched intensity, Westbrook is a former league MVP and the first player since Oscar Robertson to average a triple-double for a season — a feat he repeated multiple times. A fierce competitor and passionate leader, he has left a lasting impact on every team he’s played for through his playmaking, rebounding, and fearless drives to the rim.
